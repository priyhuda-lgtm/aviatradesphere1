Services

We offer a wide range of services, including:

✈️

24/7 AOG Support

Aviatradesphere provides a highly regarded 24/7 AOG support service. When an aircraft is grounded, every second counts. Our dedicated AOG team manages the complete resolution process — from quotation and certificate evaluation through to order processing and logistics.

🔄

Component Sales, Exchange and Loans

We maintain an extensive multi-million-dollar inventory of EASA/FAA certified rotable spare-parts. Our exchange programme allows airlines to accomplish immediate replacement of unserviceable parts without large increases in inventory and cost.

⚠️

Dangerous Goods

Aviatradesphere has vast experience supplying aircraft spare-parts classed as dangerous goods for air, road and sea transport. We prepare even the most difficult dangerous goods shipments at a moment's notice, in full compliance with IATA regulations.

💼

Buy & Sell Inventory

We have the expertise and strong capital base to help you get unwanted surplus inventory off your books. We also help airlines generate revenue from BER spare-parts. No extra charges — logistics are arranged at our expense.

🔧

Repair Management

We work in close partnership with several high quality EASA/FAA approved repair shops, offering a comprehensive repair management programme covering over 48,000 different part numbers with guaranteed turn-around-times and cost-effective repair prices.
